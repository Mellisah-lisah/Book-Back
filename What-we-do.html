What We Do

We provide books so that people can gain more and knowledge and become well equiped to tackle any challenge they will ever face

Around the world, millions of people are unable to fulfil their potential because of a basic lack of books

Through our network of partners who will support us,
we will supply around carefully selected brand new books to public, community libraries, schools, refugee camps, hospitals, prisons and universities.

Books for Everyone

Our aim is to ensure that:

    We are committed to creating a world where everyone has access to the books they need to enrich and improve their own lives We support public libraries We support education and empower teachers and librarians We would be able to enrich our community with libraries

Selecting and Sending Books

Requirements and Donations

We will require our partners to tell us the categories and quantities of books they need.
All the books we will send will be Donated from different well wishers and publishers

Distribution

The books will be stamped and distributed to our partners' libraries

Reading

Readers from all ages and stages of life will enjoy the books we send and improve their lives through reading.
